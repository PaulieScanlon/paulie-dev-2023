Mastra Studio now has a metrics dashboard that tracks model costs, latency percentiles, scores, and error counts for all your agents, tools, and workflows.

Together with a new logging system and existing tracing capabilities, these additions complete the observability framework, enabling developers to monitor aggregate performance metrics across their applications while maintaining debugging capabilities through detailed log records tied directly to execution traces.
